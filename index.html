Fizz Buzz

What is it?

Fizz Buzz is a small program which counts from one up to a specified number. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with the word "fizz" and numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with the word "buzz". For numbers that are divisible by both 3 and 5, they are replaced with "fizzbuzz". For example, the number 9 would show as "fizz", the number 20 would show as "buzz", and the number 15 would show as "fizzbuzz".
